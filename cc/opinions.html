Opinion Pieces


Welcome to the portal for the opinion pieces. Here you can read my opinion on different subjects, each piece is focused on elements relevant to the discussions from the semester. The format that these pieces are delivered in is mini essays, so these are less formal than the traditional essay like was submitted for the midterm paper.

There are three pieces to start and if I have more time I will write more, but at the moment that seems unlikely. One of these should jog your memory a bit if you recall some group events we have done in the past. Another is an extension of in class discussions, and a third which is entirely new while still being based in the course readings.

Opinion Piece #1:


A Defense of Mill

A dive into how Mill's views on liberty and utilitarianism stand up against freudian and marxist ideals.

Opinion Piece #2:


On the Man Who Killed God

A piece on Nietzsche, the man who famously declared that God was Dead.

Opinion Piece #3:


The Cycle of Colonialism

European Colonialism left a mark on the world, sparking several anti colonial movements in the mid 20th century. Today new countries are attempting to do something similar, are we doomed to repeat the cycle that is colonialism?
