Exam Materials

A solid percentage of your grade will be based on the completion of three exams, two midterms and a cumulative final exam. The midterms will be identical in format with the final being formatted differently. Clicking through will provide some details about what the format of the exam will be (approximately) and some resources to help you prepare.

Midterm 1

Worth approximately 10% of your grade.

The first midterm has approximately 25-30 multiple choice questions on it, the questions will be based off the material presented. Any material mentioned either directly in lecture, or through sources mentioned in lecture are considered fair game. The following are resources that I have made in the past to assist you in your preparation.

1.) Midterm 1 Review Guide
2.) Lecture Notes from Griffin's Lecture
3.) Griffin's Practice Midterm

The professor also typically provides a set of practice questions for the first midterm.

Final Exam

Worth approximately 30% of your grade.

The final exam has 3 sections on it, a standard multiple choice section that you should be used to, a short answer section, and a longer free response section where you'll be expected to write code, as a reminder this will all be done via pencil and paper. I have made a full length practice final exam which students have found extremely helpful and accurate in the past.
